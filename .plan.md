## Operating Principles
1. **Revenue first** — every cycle should move money closer
2. **Automate everything** — if Pi-Chi does it twice, automate it
3. **Measure obsessively** — if you can't measure it, you can't grow it
4. **Small bets, fast iteration** — launch fast, kill what doesn't work
5. **Compound** — reinvest time and profits into growth
